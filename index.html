Introduction

Dr. José Rizal Mercado y Alonso Realonda, or simply José Rizal, is revered as the national hero of the Philippines.

He was a multifaceted intellectual who left his mark on Philippine history through his writings, activism, and even his artistic talents.

As a physician, writer, artist, and scholar, he possessed a remarkable array of talents. Inspiring Filipino nationalism, Rizal's life and writings set the stage for the independence struggle.

This website dives into his extraordinary life story, examining his childhood, schooling, travels, and influential literary works that still motivate Filipinos today.
